01 — Capture

Paste anything.
AI structures it.

Brain dump, email thread, WhatsApp notes — paste it all. AI extracts every task, assigns priorities, estimates times, and groups into projects. You review before anything saves.

  • Natural language — no forms, no templates
  • AI creates projects from context automatically
  • Full preview and edit before saving
02 — Organise

Every project.
Mapped visually.

Switch between list and mind map at any time. See every project, every task, every connection — on one interactive canvas that updates in real time.

  • Interactive mind map with live task status
  • Colour-coded by project and priority
  • Click any node to edit instantly
03 — Schedule

One click.
Your week, built.

AI reads your deadlines, priorities, and time estimates, then builds an optimal week — grouping tasks by project to protect your flow state. Drag to adjust. Confirm to move.

  • Week, day, month views — like Outlook
  • Groups tasks by project to cut context switching
  • Drag-drop with confirmation to prevent accidents
04 — Improve

Track time.
Get smarter.

Log time with a live timer or in plain English. Each week AI tells you where your time actually went, what patterns it spotted, and three specific changes to make next week.

  • Live timer or “worked on X from 9 to 10:30”
  • Weekly insight score with actionable tips
  • Notification bell for overdue and due-today
