Step 01

Paste anything.
AI structures it.

Dump your notes, emails, WhatsApp messages, random thoughts. AI extracts every task, groups them into logical projects, estimates times, and assigns priorities — then shows you a preview to confirm before saving.

  • Natural language input — no forms, no templates
  • AI creates projects automatically from context
  • Review and edit everything before it saves
Step 02

Projects &
mind maps generated.

Every project gets its own board with tasks grouped by status. Switch to mind map view to see the full picture — all projects, all tasks, in a single interactive canvas.

  • Colour-coded projects with progress tracking
  • Drag to reorder, duplicate, or reassign tasks
  • Mind map view for the whole organisation
Step 03

AI builds your
focused week.

Click one button. AI reads your deadlines, priorities, and time estimates, then builds a week-view calendar grouping tasks by project to protect your flow. Drag blocks to move them — a confirmation step keeps you in control.

  • Week, day, and month views like Outlook
  • Grouped by project to minimise context switching
  • Drag-drop with confirmation — no accidental moves
Step 04

Track time. Get
smarter every week.

Log time with a live timer or describe what you worked on in plain English. Over time, AI learns your real habits — how long things actually take, when you are most focused, and where your week is being wasted.

  • Live timer or natural language time logging
  • Weekly AI insight report with actionable tips
  • Notification bell for deadlines and overdue tasks
